Welcome to Allied Recycling.

Allied Recycling is a New Jersey-based provider of innovative recycling solutions. We provide the highest quality products to our customers and our prices are highly competitive in the recycling marketplace.

Our services are of the highest efficiency and quality.

Allied recycles any product that contains ferrous or non-ferrous metals, including steel, aluminum, iron, lead, brass, copper, copper wire, junk cars, heavy trucks and equipment, buses, trailers, computers, and e-scrap. We also process paper, cardboard and plastics.

We always get the job done.

We will always pay more than the competition for your vehicles and scrap metal. Additionally, we provide various services pertaining to metals, fiber, and trucking services.
