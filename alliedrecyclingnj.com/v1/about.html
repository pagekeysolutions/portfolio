About Us

Our Company

Allied Recycling is a New Jersey based provider of innovative recycling solutions servicing the Middle Atlantic Region. We provide the highest quality products to our customers and our prices are highly competitive in the recycling marketplace.

We purchase, process, and recycle all types of metal, paper, and plastic so that it can be reused in the future and reduce the impact on our environment.

We also provide light and heavy trucking including dump trailer, crushed car trailer, freight trailer, flat deck trailer, landoll, light and heavy wrecker, flatbed, box truck, and roll off containers.

Rest assured, we have the capabilities to handle all of your recycling needs.
